# Proposal: "Study Smarter for SPM with AI"

### A 2-hour student session at SMK Vivekananda, powered by SPM AI

> Prepared for the school administration & teachers · SPM AI

---

## 1. Overview

We propose a **2-hour, hands-on session** for SMK Vivekananda's Form 4 and Form 5 students on **how to use AI responsibly and effectively to prepare for SPM**, featuring a live walkthrough of **SPM AI** — Malaysia's first AI-powered SPM revision platform.

The session is part practical study-skills workshop, part AI-literacy class, and part guided product walkthrough. Students leave with a concrete, personalised SPM study plan and the skills to use AI as a tutor — not a crutch.

**Format:** in-person, interactive · **Duration:** 2 hours · **Audience:** Form 4 & 5 (ideal 30–120 students) · **Cost to the school:** free.

---

## 2. Why this session matters

- **SPM is high-stakes and competitive** — 402,918 candidates sat SPM in 2024 (up ~8% on 2023). Every advantage counts.
- **Students are already using AI** — often badly: copy-pasting answers, trusting hallucinations, learning nothing. This session teaches them to use it *well*.
- **AI literacy is a life skill** — knowing how to learn with AI helps beyond SPM, into university and work.
- **Equity** — a free, structured platform levels the field for students who can't afford private tuition.

---

## 3. Learning objectives

By the end, students will be able to:

1. Understand what AI can and **cannot** do for studying — and how to verify its answers.
2. Use AI as a **tutor** (to explain, not to hand over answers) without becoming dependent.
3. Practise real SPM questions with **instant, marking-scheme-based feedback**.
4. Identify their **weak topics** and build a focused, week-by-week revision plan.
5. Apply proven study techniques: **active recall, spaced repetition, and timed practice**.

---

## 4. Session outline (2 hours)

| Time | Segment | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| 0:00–0:15 | **Welcome & the SPM challenge** | Icebreaker; the reality of SPM prep; how AI is changing how students learn. |
| 0:15–0:35 | **AI literacy for students** | What AI is good/bad at; spotting wrong answers; the difference between *using AI to learn* vs *cheating*; academic honesty; data & privacy. |
| 0:35–1:05 | **Live demo: SPM AI** | Students follow along on their phones: practise by topic/year, instant AI grading, ask "Cikgu AI" to explain a mistake, exam-readiness score, flashcards, streaks. |
| 1:05–1:35 | **Hands-on workshop** | Each student attempts questions in their subjects, gets them wrong on purpose, uses the AI tutor to understand, reattempts, and watches their progress rise. |
| 1:35–1:50 | **Build your SPM plan** | Students read their weak-topic report and draft a personal weekly study schedule; we teach spaced repetition & active recall. |
| 1:50–2:00 | **Q&A + next steps** | Free pilot sign-up; sponsored free access for students in need; how to keep going at home. |

*(Timings flexible; can compress to 90 minutes or extend with a longer workshop.)*

## 5. How students should prepare

**Before the session**
- Bring a **smartphone** (charged) — the platform is mobile-first.
- Know your **subjects** and roughly which topics feel hardest.
- (Optional) Create a free account in advance at the SPM AI site.

**During**
- Be willing to **get questions wrong** — that's where the AI tutoring shines.
- Ask questions; treat the AI like a patient teacher.

**After (the habit that wins SPM)**
- **15–20 minutes of daily practice** beats cramming — keep the streak.
- Each week: practise your **2 weakest topics** first.
- Always use **"Why is this the answer?"** to learn the reasoning, not just the answer.
- Do a **timed past paper** weekly as the exam nears.
